Your data is used solely for:

All your data is stored using Apple's CloudKit service in your personal iCloud account. We do not store your data on our own servers. Your data is:

- Stored in Apple's secure data centers
- Only accessible to you through your iCloud account

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers

- Access your data for any reason

Your game history and player statistics are completely private and only accessible to you through your iCloud account.

- Access your data (viewable in the app)

FishAndChips is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
