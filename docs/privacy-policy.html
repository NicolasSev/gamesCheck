Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

FishAndChips ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our poker game tracking application.

Data Collection

FishAndChips collects and stores the following information:

Information You Provide:

  • Player names and aliases (as entered by you)
  • Game details (dates, buy-ins, cashouts, game types)
  • Player profiles and statistics
  • User account information (username, email if provided)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (for iCloud synchronization)
  • CloudKit identifiers (for data synchronization)
  • App usage statistics (for crash reporting and bug fixes)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing the app's core functionality (game tracking and statistics)
  • Synchronizing your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Sending you notifications about player claims (if enabled)
Important: We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the app's functionality.

Data Storage

All your data is stored using Apple's CloudKit service in your personal iCloud account. We do not store your data on our own servers.

Your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored in Apple's secure data centers
  • Only accessible to you through your iCloud account
  • Subject to Apple's security and privacy policies

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Access your data for any reason
  • Share your data with anyone except as required by law

Your game history and player statistics are completely private and only accessible to you through your iCloud account.

Third-Party Services

FishAndChips uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple CloudKit: For data storage and synchronization (covered by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For sending notifications (covered by Apple's Privacy Policy)

No other third-party services have access to your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is viewable within the app
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual games, players, or all data by uninstalling the app and removing iCloud data from Settings
  • Export your data: Contact us for data export tools (feature planned for future update)
  • Revoke iCloud access: You can disable iCloud access for FishAndChips in iOS Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app and maintain your iCloud account. When you:

  • Uninstall the app: Local data is deleted, but iCloud data remains
  • Delete iCloud data: All data is permanently removed from Apple's servers
  • Delete specific games/players: That data is permanently removed

Children's Privacy

FishAndChips is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

We take data security seriously and rely on Apple's industry-leading security infrastructure:

  • All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Data at rest is encrypted using Apple's encryption standards
  • Access to data requires authentication through your iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your encryption keys

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Significant changes will be notified through the app or via email (if you have provided one). Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

Your data is stored in Apple's data centers, which may be located in various countries. By using FishAndChips, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

Compliance

This app and our privacy practices comply with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
